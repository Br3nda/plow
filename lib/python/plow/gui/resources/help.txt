---
CLUSTER.NAME
The custer name. All custers must have a unique name.
---
CLUSTER.TAGS
All nodes in a cluster inherit the cluster tags.

Cluster tags are used to match to particular layers to nodes during the dispatch process.  A layer must have the correct cluster tags to get procs.  
---
CLUSTER.LOCKED
Nodes in locked clusters are not dispatched.
---
CLUSTER.DEFAULT
The default cluster is where newly discovered nodes will congregate awaiting to be categorized into their permanent cluster.
